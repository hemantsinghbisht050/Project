INTRODUCTION

On Dec. 31, 2019, Chinese authorities alerted the World Health Organization of pneumonia cases in Wuhan City, Hubei province, China, with an unknown cause.
What started as a mystery disease was first referred to as 2019-nCoV and then named COVID-19.Since then,on 30th July worldwide COVID-19 confirmed cases has reached 197 Millions and Deaths has reached 4.2 Millions
As part of this Visualization we will look at the Daily cases and Death in Chicago area recorded since 10th March 2020 till 29th July 2021.In the end we will compare Chicago's case and Death numbers with Illinois's Case and death numbers.
